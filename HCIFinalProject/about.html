About our company

History

VOrskins' journey to become one of Indonesia's most leading vegan, non-cruelty, and non-toxic skincare and bodycare company started in 2022.

Our founders, Reyga Joevan Pratama and Joaquin Arlind was finding a solution to Indonesia's unavailability of good skincare and bodycare that was safe to use by all people including vegans.

VOrskins believes if there is a platform that can provide customers' need of skincare and bodycare, then they don't have to use really cheap and unknown products that can harm the skin of the customer.

VOrskins' dream of reducing animal cruelty in the making of skincare and bodycare products will keep on until generations to come.

Vision

  • Help create more plant-based skincare and bodycare for vegans by using all-natural ingredients
  • Collaborate with other beauty product companies to tackle Indonesia's beauty industry problems
  • Make beauty products accessible for all people price-wise and allergy-wise

Mission

  • Provide vegan, cruelty-free, and non-toxic skincare and bodycare for people
  • To support cruelty-free beauty products and stop the abuse of animals for the sake of our beauty
  • Display only high quality and trusted skincare and bodycare products on the website
